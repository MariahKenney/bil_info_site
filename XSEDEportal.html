Create an XSEDE Portal Account

Overview

An XSEDE portal account is required to submit data to the Brain Image Library. The steps to create an XSEDE Portal Account are:
  1. Visit the XSEDE Portal Website: portal.xsede.org
  2. Enter the Required Information
  3. Enter security code sent in email
  4. Enter Additional Information

How to Create an XSEDE Portal Account

  1. Visit the XSEDE Portal Website: To get an XSEDE portal ID, visit portal.xsede.org and click on the blue Create Account button on the left hand side of the screen.
